The Mother finds Par─Бс╣Еku┼Ыa N─Бyak─л, seated like a discourser, with the fingers in an instructional pose and declaring that it is she that created the whole world, that she would become the world herself and so on. The bewildered mother, when queried by her kinsmen says that the N─Бyak─л is perhaps possessed by the Supreme Lord who is the Inner Self of all beings. Every song in this decad is in two distinct parts, namely, the narration of the N─Бyak─лтАЩs God-vision (man identified with God), followed by the Mother’s suggestion that it is perhaps God who has taken possession of the N─Бyak─л, that speaks through her. There is of course, a parallel for this kind of behaviour of the N─Бyak─л in ┼Ъr─л Viс╣гс╣Зu Pur─Бс╣Зa, V-13-23. In the midst of that rapturous balldance, the classical тАШR─Бsakr─лс╕Н─БтАЩ, Lord Kс╣Ыс╣гс╣Зa suddenly withdrew Himself from the Gop─лs of Vс╣Ыnd─Бvan, who were literally bursting with joy in the company of the Divine cowherd boy, who danced in between every two girls, assuming ubiquity to the extent needed. The LordтАЩs sudden disappearance was motivated by His desire to stabilise the Gop─лsтАЩ God-enjoyment, which was at its highest ebb at that crucial moment, much beyond their capacity to bear. Some time later, He appeared in their midst back again, assuming afar more enchanting Form than before, as if to make amends for the pangs of separation inflicted on them. But what happened to the Gop─лs during this interval? They were sunk deep in dejection, expressed by them in immortal songs, known as тАШGopik─Б-G─лt─БтАЩ, Some of the Gop─лs also resorted to the technique of impersonation of ┼Ъr─л Kс╣Ыс╣гс╣Зa, so as to sustain themselves, as brought out in the ┼Ъloka of ┼Ъr─л Viс╣гс╣Зu Pur─Бс╣Зa, referred to above. There was a Gop─л claiming to be none other than Kс╣Ыс╣гс╣Зa and calling upon her mates to verify the truth of her statement from her gait.

Another asserted that she was Kс╣Ыс╣гс╣Зa and started playing on the flute; yet another, posing as Kс╣Ыс╣гс╣Зa, addressed a Gop─л as:

тАЬYou, deadly snake, stay, or I will kill youтАЭ.

That other Gop─л deigned to play the role of K─Бliyaс╣Й, the snake, so that she could be smeared with the dust from the feet of Kс╣Ыс╣гс╣Зa (the Gop─л simulating Kс╣Ыс╣гс╣Зa). With appropriate gesticulations and a great deal of gusto, a Gop─л feigning as Kс╣Ыс╣гс╣Зa, performed feats such as holding Mount Govardhan aloft, slaying of Dhenuk─Бsura and so on. By such means, these Gopis managed to sustain themselves without succumbing to the pangs of separation from ┼Ъr─л Kс╣Ыс╣гс╣Зa. There is also the example set by ┼Ъr─л ─Ас╣Зс╣н─Бс╕╖ through her immortal тАЬTirupp─БvaiтАЭ, the daughter of a high class Brahmin (Viс╣гс╣Зucitta) adopting, by inclination, the pastoral life of the shepherds and becoming one of them.
